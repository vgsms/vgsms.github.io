Made with Love

Found and owned by a 9 years old student Yusuf YILDIRIM with a interesting story. He wanted a GSM Shield but it was too expensive for their family and SIM card is being risked in cheap sheilds. He had this idea when he was 9 and he made the first GSM Sheildless call in the history in 2020.

Better than Sheilds

Using VirtualGSMS on any old or new smartphone is; Safer (with SIM card protection), Faster, Cheaper, Customizable, Good for avoiding E-Wastes, More sensors, More use-cases, Less IMEI problems, Better Connection, and more.

Works on Most Devices

With the Android 4+ support, VirtualGSMS has a wide range of devices to work. You can use your old phones - it can work on almost every any Android device! Also, IOS support is planned to launch in 2025 too!
